About Me

Self-teach programming and begin working on personal projects

Towards the end of 2017 began learning web coding (FreeCodeCamp) and Java programming (Revature online curriculum). As my interest grew I learned more extensive skills and frameworks including Node/Express.js, SQL/ORM and React.

Left work to Study an MBA

After 4 years working as a financial analyst then finance manager across London and Brighton, I left Amex to embark on an MBA at London Business School, looking to learn more about what kind of work interests me.

Started working in Financial Analysis

After a 6 month internship as a quantitative research assistant (including my first exposure to a command line interface via Stata), went on to join American Express as a financial analyst in London.

Completed Bachelors' and Masters' Degrees in Economics

Studied Economics and Natural Sciences at Trinity College, Cambridge, followed by a 1 year MSc in Development Economics at SOAS.
